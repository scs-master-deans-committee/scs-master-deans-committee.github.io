Frequently Asked Questions

MAC


What's the difference between MAC and GSA?
GSA (Graduate Student Assembly):
  • Focuses on Graduate Student issues, and includes representation from Master's students across the University
  • Hosts large scale events and initiatives
  • Their relationship with administration is broader, less direct and more complicated
  • Diffused scope of work
MAC (Master's Advisory Committee):
  • Hyper-focused on Master's Student issues within SCS
  • Hosts targeted events
  • Listens and relays information to the Dean in form of actionable suggestions, has a very direct access to administration
  • Specific charge on academic diffussion
How can I join the Committee?
We are contiuously looking for new members to help us improve the experience for all master students! If you want to get involved, the first step is sending us an email at masters-advisory-committee@cs.cmu.edu and we will get back to you within 3 business days with the next steps.
