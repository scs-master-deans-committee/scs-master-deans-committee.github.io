SCS Dean's Masters Advisory Committee

The committee serves to better student life for master’s students in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University by providing feedback to the Dean and spearheading initiatives in the following areas

  • Resources
  • Mental Health
  • Culture and Atmosphere Academics
  • Interdepartment Communication Personal Wellness
